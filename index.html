What is Stoicism?



Stoicism is a school of thought. Not a religion.

When and Where Did It Originate?



Stoicism was founded in Athens by Zeno of Citium. It was influenced by Socrates and the Cynics, and it engaged in vigorous debates various other schools of philosophy.
